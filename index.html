Intermission

Sound

comes in periodic waves

Sound comes in periodic waves periodicity is perceived as pitch

Frequency

number of times the wave pattern repeats every second, meassured in Hz

Pitch is meassured in frequency (number of times the wave pattern repeats every second) in hertz

Pitch

human perception of periodicity of the soundwave

double the wave (higher freq) -> same note, pitch an octave higher half the wave (lower freq) -> same note, pitch an octave lower

sample frame

length, sampleRate

length = size in sample frames

sampleRate = number of sample frames per second

An AudioBuffer takes as its parameters a number of channels (1 for mono, 2 for stereo, etc), a length, meaning the number of sample frames inside the buffer, and a sample rate, which is the number of sample frames played per second.

/Intermission
